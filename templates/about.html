The goal of Database for Medication Safety in Geriatric Patients (DMSG) is to:

  • Gives access to both BEERS criteria and STOPP/START criteria recommendation for drug classes and medications.
  • Allows healthcare professional from the same healthcare institute to share notes with each other regarding each drug or drugclass recommendation to enhace patient care.

Guidelines:

BEERS criteria and STOPP/START criteria are two most prominent guidelines for healthcare professionals to help improve the safety of prescribing medications for older adults. BEERS criteria is a guideline published by American Geriatric Society in 2015 to identify potentially inappropriate medication use in geriatric population. You can click here to view the full publication of BEERS criteria.
